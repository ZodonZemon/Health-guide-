📌 Pay Attention

Method 🚫 Why? 🤔
❌ Do not dissolve the tablet in lukewarm water or juice Neem tablets have a special coating. Their effectiveness may decrease if they dissolve quickly. 💊
❌ Do not chew the tablets Chewing can make it taste very bitter and may cause stomach irritation. 😖

🥣 Best Method (Example)

    1. Take 1-2 Neem tablets first thing in the morning. 🌞

    2. Drink 150-200 ml of lukewarm water with it. (The water should be warm enough for you to drink easily). 💧

    3. If you have a specific problem (like a skin allergy or need to purify your blood), take 10-15 ml of Giloy juice or Tulsi water after the Neem tablet. ✨

    4. To allow the medicine to be absorbed well into the body, do not eat anything for 30 minutes. ⏳
